Overview

I built two new sites for the Archdiocese of St. Louis: a lightweight site for the Fr. Scheid Players (ticketing and ad sales), and a more complex WordPress site for the joint offices of Marriage Preparation and Family Life, including event registration and resource pages.

  • Ticket & ad purchase flow for a seasonal play (simple, quick, & mobile friendly)
  • WordPress site built from the ground up for two offices, with shared elements
  • Clear to navigate informational site with included event & class registration

Process & Decisions

For the play site, I focused on the few tasks visitors actually need: check dates, buy tickets, and purchase program ads. The joint offices site separates resources for both engaged and married couples and family life—while while sharing an easy navigation system for no matter who is visiting the site.

Focus Areas

  • Easy to follow backend system for the Office Manager and Directors
