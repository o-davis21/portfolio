About Me

I’m Owen Davis, a Human-Centered Computing student at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) with hands-on experience building production-level websites for large nonprofit organizations. I’ve contributed to live platforms across multiple dioceses, working within structured content management systems and real-world deployment environments.


My focus is building responsive, accessible, and intuitive web experiences that prioritize clarity and usability. With a foundation in both technical implementation and user-centered design principles, I approach development as both an engineering and design discipline.


Outside of professional work, I design and fabricate 3D printed armor and props. I in particularly specialize in Star Wars prop and costume refabrication.
