Context & Constraints

I completed a spring internship with the Youth Faith Formation that led to a web developer role during the end of my Senior year while in high school. Following a department restructuring, prior developed sites were retired or reworked. This case study focuses on how I approached the development and collaboration rather than on the now‑retired implementations.

Role

Web Developer

Department

Youth Faith Formation

Platforms

WordPress

What I Did

  • Re-envisioned the front end of the Department's website with the Executive Director. This included all individual office sites within it.
  • Worked individually with each Director to bring their vision to life for their office while maintaining an overall similar Department styling.
  • Coordinated meetings between the Executive Director, the Web Development team for the Archdiocese, and myself to start implementing the new Department styling across the entire Archdiocese website.
